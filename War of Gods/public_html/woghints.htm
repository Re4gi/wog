#1 BASIC COMMANDS


movement (basic commands to movement from one room to another):
n - north
s - south
w - west
e - east
nw - northwest
ne - northeast
sw - southwest
se - southeast
u - up
d - down

score and etc.:
% - repeats last command
sc - score, show basic information about character
ss - short score, show your hp, fp, mana a exp to next level
at or att - attributes, show your offense, dodge, parry, absorbage, mood of your god, starving, weight, spells on you
donation - you will received basic items (work only for newbies - until level 5)
i - inventory, shows your inventory
keep <item> - this will prevent from selling, dropping or junking a item; keep <item> again to remove keep for this item
keep list - show items, which you have in keep list
keep clear - clears keep list
eq - equipment, shows your equipment
session - shows session summary, time of session, experince and money gained during session
skills - shows your learned skills
help all skills - shows all skills of your class
help all abilities - shows all abilities of your class
help all spells - shows all spells of your class
help all traits - shows all traits of your class
help all <class> skills - shows all skills of selected class
help all <class> abilities - shows all abilities of selected class
help all <class> spells - shows all spells of selected class
help all <class> traits - shows all traits of selected class
who - shows online players (does not show incognitos and enemy players) and sum of all players on both sides
who zone - shows online players in zone (available on level 11+)
where - shows players country
wl - shows warlist
war - shows outposts and which side has claimed them
pk - shows hunter and trophy of the day
savings - shows resistance and imunities of your character
quests - shows quests you gained and which are in progress
trophy - shows numbers and names of creatures you have killed
news - shows you news in wog
save - save your character (this is done also automatically)

communication:
sh - shout, this will hear every player of your side
y - yell, this will hear every players (enemies too) in zone and neareast
t - tell, this will hear only player who recieve tell
say or ' - this will hear each player in your room
whisper - this will hear only player who recieve whisper, but players in room see that you are whispering, also sleeping player
psay - this will hear each partymember and each player in the room where is party member
channel - there are few channels, newbie, class (each class has own), arena (available on level 20+), and clan (available if you are in clan)
messages - show you last 10 communication messages

party:
party <name> - create party named <name> (party must create highest level)
disband party - disband party, can only leader of party
leave party  - you leave party
dismiss <name> - dismiss <name> from party, can only leader of party
pwho - partywho - show members in party, their hp, fp, mana, level and distance
psay - communication in party (see psay in communication section)
recruit <name> or all - will be recruit <name> or all who will follow you to party
follow <name> - you will start or stop follow <name> player, you must follow party leader to join the party

equipment:
eq - equipment, eq - equipment, show you your equipment
equ - equip, will equip you first of each equipmnet from your invetory
wi - wield, wield weapon in hands
we - wear equip on you
i - invetory, show your inventory
hold or light or use <torch> will be light torch

misc:
k - kill, start fight
flee - run from fight
con - consider, you can consider monster
do - you can do with do multialias, but no in pk
campfire - will set up campfire for better regeneration, but you need wood
scout - you can seen mobs in other room
zone <name> - this get more about <name> zone
h zones - will show list of zones
quaff <potion> - quaffs a potion

commands in shop:
list - show availe items
buy - buy items from shop
sell - sell items to shop
value - you can see vaule of item in inventory
browse - you can see descibe of items in shop
repair - you can repair your damaged equipment at weaponer/armourer/blacksmith
resize - you can resize equipment to your size at armourer/leather shop

commands in rent:
offer - show you rent cost
rent - rent your character
deposit - deposit equipment in storage
withdraw - withdraw equipment from storage
list - show you your equipment storage
browse - you can see describe of equipment in storage

commands in bank:
deposit - deposits money to your bank account
withdraw - withdraws money from your bank account
balance - shows your current amount of money in bank

commands in alchemist shop:
list - show availe items
buy - buy items from shop
sell - sell items to shop
value - you can see vaule of item in inventory
browse - you can see descibe of items in shop
brew - shows list of avalaible potions to brew at alchemist
brew <spell> - alchemist will brew potion with chosen spell

commands in church:
list - show you avalible spells
(bless, prayer, cure medium wounds, cure heavy wounds, cure blindness, dispel magic and restore)

commands in guild:
list - show you list of available features (skills, spells, abilities and traits)
learn - learn a feature (skill, spell, ability and trait) (for learming points)
improve - improve a primary staty if you have stat point
forget - forget a trait (must be 40+; you can forgot only 2 traits)
advance - advance to next level
You can advance when you have 0 exps to next level or exps in negative and 0 travelpoints remaining in score.
You can lose travelpoints when you travel between zones.
